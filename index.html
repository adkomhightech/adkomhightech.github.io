About Adkom

Adkom was established with the vision of building high-quality laboratory analytical instruments that advance research and industrial applications. As a startup, we are driven by the goal of creating reliable, precise, and durable equipment that supports science and innovation.

Our focus areas include the development of lab ovens, stability chambers, incubators, and furnaces. Each instrument is designed with attention to accuracy and long-term performance, ensuring it meets the demanding requirements of modern laboratories.

Innovation and quality are central to our approach. We continually explore new ideas, materials, and technologies to improve usability, efficiency, and sustainability in laboratory environments.

Adkom is at the beginning of its journey, but our mission is clear: to become a trusted name in laboratory analytical instruments by setting new benchmarks for reliability and performance.
